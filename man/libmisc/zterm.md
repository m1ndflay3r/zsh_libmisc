## zterm - *tmux-like <zsh/zpty> wrapper*

*includes attach / detach functionality, multiple named terms, etc etc*

------------------------------------

**example:**

  *create new term*

    zterm new <termname>

  *attach to term*

    zterm att <termname>

  *close term*

    zterm clo <termname>

  *send to term*

    zterm sen <termname> "somecommand"

  *read term*

    zterm rec <termname>

  *help*

    zterm --help

------------------------------------

*detach from attached term using 'CTRL+\'*
